Storyteller

I've spent my life falling in love with stories that bring me to tears, make me nostalgic for a time that did not happen (or at least not yet), and find a way to make me laugh, sometimes all on the same page.

Imagine my surprise and excitement when ideas started asking me to be their writer, and my joy when I realized I have stories to share with the world that will do all those things I love.
